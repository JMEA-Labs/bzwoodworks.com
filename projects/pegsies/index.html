Pegsies Wooden Peg Dolls

In 2020, with two young daughters and an unexpected amount of free time, our family decided to create a wood based creative and playful doll option. We saw so many toys on the market that simply wasted a regrettable amount of plastic and felt we had some ideas to offer. Pegsies, as its own brand and company, has grown over the years found its way into homes, schools, and churches in all 50 states and more than 10 countries.
